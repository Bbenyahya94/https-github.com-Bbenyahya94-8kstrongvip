Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my subscription?

After payment, you will receive your Xtream Codes (Server URL, Username, Password) via email or WhatsApp. Use these credentials in any IPTV player.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade to a longer plan anytime. Contact our support team, and we'll help you upgrade with prorated pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, credit/debit cards, cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, USDT), and bank transfers for larger packages.

Does my subscription auto-renew?

No auto-renewal. You will be notified before your subscription expires. Simply contact us to renew when you're ready.

Can I use on multiple devices?

Yes! The number of simultaneous connections depends on your plan. 1 connection for 1-3 months, 2 connections for 6-12 months.

Our support team is available 24/7 via Telegram and WhatsApp. We typically respond within 2 hours.
