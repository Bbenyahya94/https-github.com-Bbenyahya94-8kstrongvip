Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your 8K Strong VIP experience. Read our refund policy below to understand your rights and our commitment to customer satisfaction.

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you are not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days of your purchase, you may request a full refund.

1. Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following conditions:

  • Refund request must be submitted within 7 days of the original purchase date
  • You have not violated our Terms of Service
  • You have a valid reason for the refund request
  • You have not already received a refund for the same subscription

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refund:

  • Reseller Credits: Credits purchased for the reseller program are non-refundable but can be converted to subscriptions
  • Subscriptions older than 7 days: Refund requests received after 7 days will not be processed
  • Promotional or discounted plans: Special offer plans are final sale
  • Auto-renewals: Renewal payments are non-refundable unless requested within 7 days of the renewal date
  • Partial usage: No partial refunds for unused portions of a subscription

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with the following information:

  • Your account email address
  • Order number or payment reference
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for refund request

You can submit your refund request through any of these channels:

  • Telegram: @8kstrong_support
  • WhatsApp: +212 670 969 192
  • Email: support@8kstrongvip.com

4. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after approval
  • The refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund is processed
  • Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take additional 2-5 days for the refund to appear in your account

5. Refund for Reseller Credits

Reseller credits are non-refundable. However, they can be:

  • Converted to subscription months (1 Credit = 1 Month)
  • Transferred to another reseller account
  • Used for future customer subscriptions

If you have unused credits and wish to stop your reseller business, please contact support to discuss your options.

6. Chargebacks and Disputes

We encourage customers to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback or dispute. If a chargeback is filed:

  • Your account will be suspended immediately
  • You will lose access to our services
  • Your email and IP address may be blacklisted
  • You will be responsible for any chargeback fees incurred

We are always happy to resolve any issues directly. Please contact us first – we will work with you to find a fair solution.

7. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. The refund policy in effect at the time of your purchase will apply to your transaction.
