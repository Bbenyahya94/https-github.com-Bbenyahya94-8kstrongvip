IPTV Setup Guide

Complete step-by-step guide to install 8K Strong VIP on all your devices. Choose your device below and follow the instructions.

Firestick / Fire TV

1
Install Downloader App
Go to Amazon App Store, search for "Downloader", install it. Then go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → Enable Apps from Unknown Sources.
2
Download 8K Player
Open Downloader app, enter one of these codes:
993968 (Fast 8K Player v6) or 405663 (8K Player VIP v3.8)
Click Go and wait for download.
Downloader Codes: 993968 | 405663
3
Install and Login
After download, click Install, then Open. Enter your Xtream Codes (Server URL, Username, Password) or M3U URL. Start watching!

Android TV / Phone

1
Download APK
Visit our Downloader page and download the latest 8K Player APK. Or use Downloader app with code: 993968.
2
Install APK
Open the downloaded file, click Install. If prompted, allow installation from unknown sources (Settings → Security → Unknown Sources).
3
Login & Enjoy
Open 8K Player, select "Login with Xtream Codes", enter your Server URL, Username, and Password. Start streaming!

iPhone / iPad / Apple TV

1
Download IPTV App
Go to App Store, search and install GSE Smart IPTV or IPTV Smarters Player.
2
Add Your Playlist
Open the app, select "Add Playlist" or "Login with Xtream Codes". Enter your Server URL, Username, and Password.
3
Start Watching
Your channels will load automatically. Enjoy 4K/8K streaming on your iOS device!

Samsung / LG Smart TV

1
Install Smart IPTV App
Samsung: Smart Hub → Search "Smart IPTV" → Install. LG: LG Content Store → Search "Smart IPTV" → Install.
2
Get MAC Address
Open the app, note down your MAC address shown on screen.
3
Upload Playlist
Go to siptv.app/mylist on your phone or computer, enter MAC address and your M3U URL or Xtream Codes. Click Send. Restart app.

MAG Box

1
Access Settings
Press Settings button on remote, go to System Settings → Servers → Portals.
2
Enter Portal URL
Enter your Portal URL (provided after subscription). Save and reboot the device.
3
Start Watching
After reboot, your IPTV service will load automatically. Enjoy!

Windows / macOS

1
Download 8K Player
Visit our Downloader page and download 8K Player for Windows or macOS.
2
Install the App
Run the installer and follow the setup instructions. Launch 8K Player.
3
Login & Watch
Enter your Xtream Codes (Server URL, Username, Password) and start streaming in 4K/8K quality.

Enigma2 / Dreambox

1
Install Plugin
Install JediMakerXtream or XStreamity plugin on your Enigma2 device.
2
Add Playlist
Enter your Xtream Codes (Server URL, Username, Password) in the plugin settings.
3
Restart & Enjoy
Restart your Enigma2 device. Your channels will appear in the bouquets.

Quick Tips & Troubleshooting

Buffering Issues

Use our built-in VPN to bypass ISP throttling. Restart your router and device. Check your internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps for 4K).

Login Problems

Make sure you're using correct credentials (Server URL, Username, Password). Contact support if you forgot your details.

Channels Not Loading

Clear app cache, restart the app, or try a different IPTV player. Contact support if issue persists.

Installation Issues

Enable "Unknown Sources" in settings. Use Downloader codes: 993968 or 405663. Disable any VPN during installation.
