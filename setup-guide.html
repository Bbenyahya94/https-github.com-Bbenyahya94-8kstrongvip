IPTV Setup Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to set up 8K Strong IPTV on your device

Firestick / Fire TV

  1. Go to Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options
  2. Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources"
  3. Search for and install "Downloader" from the App Store
  4. Open Downloader and enter the IPTV app URL (TiviMate, IPTV Smarters)
  5. Install the app and open it
  6. Enter your M3U URL or Xtream Codes credentials
  7. Start watching!
Recommended app: TiviMate for best experience

Android Phone/Tablet

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Search for "IPTV Smarters Pro" or "TiviMate"
  3. Install the app
  4. Open the app and select "Login with Xtream Codes" or "Load M3U"
  5. Enter your credentials or M3U URL
  6. Enjoy your channels!
IPTV Smarters Pro is free and easy to use

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open App Store
  2. Search for "GSE IPTV" or "IPTV Smarters"
  3. Download and install the app
  4. Open the app and tap "Add Playlist"
  5. Select "Remote Playlist" and paste your M3U URL
  6. Your channels will load automatically
GSE IPTV is highly recommended for iOS

Smart TV (Samsung/LG)

  1. Open the Smart TV app store (Samsung Apps / LG Content Store)
  2. Search for "Smart IPTV" or "SS IPTV"
  3. Install the app
  4. Open the app and note your device MAC address
  5. Upload your playlist to the app's website using the MAC address
  6. Restart the app to see your channels
Smart IPTV requires one-time payment for premium features

Windows / Mac

  1. Download VLC Media Player from videolan.org (free)
  2. Open VLC and go to Media → Open Network Stream
  3. Paste your M3U URL and click Play
  4. To browse channels, click View → Playlist
  5. Alternatively, download IPTV Smarters for Windows
VLC is free and works on all computers

Enigma2 / MAG Box

  1. Access your device portal via browser (IP address)
  2. Go to Settings → Servers → Portals
  3. Add new portal with your Xtream Codes server URL
  4. Enter your username and password
  5. Save and restart the device
  6. Channels will load automatically
Use Xtream Codes format for best compatibility
