8K Strong VIP respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). This policy outlines how copyright infringement claims are handled.

DMCA Compliance

We are committed to responding to notices of alleged copyright infringement that comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act. If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used or displayed on our website or service in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, please contact our designated DMCA agent.

1. Designated DMCA Agent

All copyright infringement notices should be sent to our designated DMCA agent:

2. Required Information for DMCA Notice

To file a valid DMCA notice, please provide the following information:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  • Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, including the URL or other specific location
  • Your contact information (name, address, telephone number, and email address)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner
  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, under penalty of perjury

Please note that you may be liable for damages (including costs and attorneys' fees) if you materially misrepresent that material is infringing your copyrights.

3. Counter-Notification Procedure

If you believe that your material was removed or disabled by mistake, you may submit a counter-notification. A valid counter-notification must include:

  • Your physical or electronic signature
  • Identification of the material that was removed and the location where it appeared before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief that the material was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification
  • Your name, address, telephone number, and email address
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court in your judicial district

4. Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA, we reserve the right to terminate accounts of users who are repeat infringers of copyright law. This policy applies to all users, including subscribers, resellers, and content uploaders. We may also terminate accounts at our sole discretion, with or without warning.

5. Third-Party Content

8K Strong VIP provides IPTV streaming services. We do not host, upload, or store any copyrighted content on our servers. Our service provides access to content through third-party sources. If you believe any content accessed through our service infringes your copyright, please contact us with the information above.

6. Fair Use Disclaimer

Copyrighted content displayed on our service may be used for informational, educational, or entertainment purposes under the fair use doctrine. However, we take copyright infringement seriously and will respond promptly to valid DMCA notices.

7. Limitation of Liability

8K Strong VIP acts as a service provider and is not liable for any copyright infringement by its users. We will promptly remove or disable access to allegedly infringing content upon receipt of a valid DMCA notice.
